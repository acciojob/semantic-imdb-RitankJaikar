Whiplash

Whiplash follows Andrew, a young jazz drummer at a prestigious music school, driven by the intense and abusive mentorship of his instructor, Fletcher, to pursue perfection. The film explores the psychological and physical tolls of ambition, pushing the boundaries between greatness and self-destruction.
Actors: Miles Teller stars as Andrew Neiman, the ambitious drummer. J.K. Simmons portrays the ruthless instructor, Terence Fletcher.
